# Font Specifications

## Fonts Used (Refer to Variables)

### Noto Serif JP (For Titles – Serif Font)

* SemiBold

### Noto Sans JP (For Body Text – Sans Serif Font)

* Bold / Medium / Regular

> Default weight is Medium.
> Regular is used in areas such as the 18px supplementary text beneath titles, but it is acceptable to standardize everything to Medium if preferred.
>
> Bold is used for Privacy Policy titles and emphasized text.

### EB Garamond (For English Text and Numbers)

* Medium / Regular

> Default weight is Medium.
> Regular is used for labels such as FLOW01, etc.

All fonts are loaded via **Google Fonts**.

### Default Font Sizes

* Body text: 16px
* Some supplementary text uses 14px

## Sample Privacy Policy Text

### Privacy Policy

Kuranomiya Purchase Store (hereinafter referred to as "the Store") establishes the following Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "this Policy") regarding the handling of users' personal information in the services provided through this website.

### Article 1 (Personal Information)

The Store complies with laws and regulations concerning the protection of personal information and other applicable standards.

"Personal Information" refers to information such as a person's name, telephone number, address, and any other information capable of identifying a specific individual, including information that can be easily matched with other data to identify a specific individual.

### Article 2 (Method of Collecting Personal Information)

The Store may request personal information such as name, email address, and telephone number when users utilize the services.

Additionally, transaction records and payment-related information containing users' personal information may be collected from business partners (including information providers, advertisers, advertising distributors, etc.; collectively referred to as "Partners").

### Article 3 (Purpose of Collection and Use)

Personal information is collected and used for the following purposes:

* Identity verification during the purchase and sale of secondhand goods in accordance with the Secondhand Articles Dealer Act
* Providing and operating Store services
* Responding to user inquiries (including identity verification)
* Sending information regarding new features, updates, campaigns, and other services offered by the Store
* Contacting users regarding maintenance or important notices
* Identifying users who violate terms of service or attempt fraudulent or improper use
* Allowing users to view, update, or delete registration information and review usage history
* Purposes incidental to the above

### Article 4 (Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties)

#### 1.

The Store will not provide personal information to third parties without prior consent except in the following circumstances:

* When necessary to protect life, body, or property and obtaining consent is difficult
* When disclosure recipients and disclosed information have been specified and customer consent has been obtained
* When cooperation with law enforcement or government agencies is legally required and obtaining consent could impede those duties

#### 2.

The following recipients are not considered third parties:

* Contractors entrusted with handling personal information necessary for payment processing, shipping arrangements, or fulfillment of service purposes
* Successors receiving information through mergers or other business transfers

### Article 5 (Correction and Deletion of Personal Information)

Users may request correction, addition, or deletion of personal information held by the Store if it is inaccurate.

If the Store determines such a request is justified, the requested correction shall be made without delay.

Users will be notified without delay whether corrections are made or denied.
